Simple Tactics
==============

Move duders around and have them hit one another.

Duder
-----

    templ = require "./duder_template"

    Duder = (I={}, self=Model(I)) ->
      Object.defaults I,
        name: "Duder Jr."
        hp: 3
        hpMax: 3

      self.observeAll()

      return self

    guys = [
      Duder()
      Duder()
      Duder()
    ]

    $('body').append templ(guys[0])
